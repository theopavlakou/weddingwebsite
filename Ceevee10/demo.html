How it all started...

Theo and Pamela met at a Christian Union event one lovely day in October at Imperial College London. They spoke for a while, and Theo then realised he left his bag somewhere and disappeared into the night. This, they both agree on. Now, as to what happens next, you'll get a different story depending on who you ask.

According to Theo, Pamela then searched for him relentlessly, asking everyone at church where the stalion with the red jumper and statuesque features was, but to her disappointment, he was nowhere to be found. Months later, thanks to Theo's friend, Benedict Kerres, Pamela was able to locate Theo once again. She asked Benedict for Theo's number, Facebook, email, id number, passport number, etc. (to ensure that she would never lose it again) and then contacted Theo. She told him how she couldn't stop thinking of him from the day that they had met and how little she could sleep because of it. Intruiged by this, Theo then asked her out to get to know her a little better. The first date went well and so a subsequent one followed. This continued for 47 dates and then Theo and Pamela finally had their first kiss. The rest was history and here we are today (well, the middle part wasn't all flowers and candy but pshht...).

Theo

The man who stole her heart

Theo hails from Cyprus but was born in Liverpool, England. He is a simple man, enjoying a quiet night in with his lovely lady, time with friends, cooking, singing and photography. He likes to work out at the gym but he was never any good at sports, which is probably why he decided to study EEE at Imperial where he would be surrounded by people who, like him, could not kick a ball straight. From the description and the picture, it is clear why Pamela fell for him.

Pamela

The girl who's heart was stolen

Coming from Brazil, Pamela loves dancing and singing. She prefers to do it alone at home with sound proof walls because she believes that no one ought to hear her voice and become green with envy, but on occasion Theo gets the privilege of witnessing her in her glory. She enjoys spending time with her knight in shining armour, shouting at the football players half way across the world while she watches them on television, traveling and photography.
